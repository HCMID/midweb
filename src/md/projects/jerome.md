# *Chronicles* of Jerome #

We are beginning work on two manuscripts of the *Chronicles*:

- Geneva Library, Ms. lat. 49 (late 15th century).
- St. Gallen Kantonsbibliothek, Vadianische Sammlung 298 (early 15th century).
